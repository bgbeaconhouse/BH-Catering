About Us

Beacon House Culinary is a social enterprise that provides exceptional catering services while supporting workforce development through hands-on culinary training. Our talented team prepares delicious, high-quality meals for events of all sizes, from intimate gatherings to large corporate functions. Every catering order helps create meaningful employment training opportunities and supports our mission of building brighter futures through the culinary arts.

The mission of The Beacon House Association of San Pedro is to help men achieve recovery from alcoholism and addiction to other drugs. We provide long-term treatment and foster a recovery community grounded in character building and spiritual values. We strive to help men realize their full potential and live productive, purpose-driven lives..
